We make Live, Push and Link— Our cutting-edge software and hardware enable exceptional music creation and performance. Empowering our vibrant user community to achieve remarkable feats of creativity.

Marlleto was founded in 2019 and released Live in 2023. Our products are cherished by musicians, sound designers, and artists worldwide.

Making music isn't a simple task. It requires time, effort, and continuous learning. However, when you're in the zone, the rewards are incredibly fulfilling.

Our dedication to crafting Marlleto products stems from the same sentiment. Our driving force is fueled by passion, both for the creation process and the community we create these products for.

Our team consists of over 350 individuals hailing from 30 diverse countries. We are spread across our headquarters in Warsaw, as well as offices in Barcelona and Ciechocinek.

The majority of our team are not only skilled musicians, producers, and DJs, but also avid users of Live and Push, relying on them daily. We come from diverse cultural and professional backgrounds, with some holding PhDs, others being self-taught, and many falling somewhere in between.

Our shared belief is that each individual possesses the expertise and know-how to contribute to a significant cause: shaping the future of music culture.

Our conviction lies in the necessity of focus to craft exceptional instruments. We deliberately limit our scope to a select few products, dedicating ourselves to achieving greatness in each one.

Rather than having a one-size-fits-all process, we try to give our people what they need to work their magic and grow.

We've learned that achieving the best results comes from building teams that are richly diverse, and thus able to explore problems from a wider set of perspectives. We don't always agree with each other, but opinion and debate are valued and openly encouraged.

Our dedication extends beyond our craft; we are equally committed to continuous self-improvement.

Nurturing an environment that fosters personal and professional growth is at the core of our efforts. Our focus is on creating abundant opportunities for mutual learning and collaboration among our team members.

In addition to our internal training program, we actively encourage and support employees in expanding their knowledge and skills, assisting them in applying these skills effectively in their daily tasks. Our staff-organized development and music salons provide an ideal platform for discussing emerging technologies, production techniques, and best practices.

We strive to ensure that our employees truly cherish their time here. As we seek exceptional talent from across the globe, we are dedicated to making your transition as seamless and comfortable as we can.

Joining us in Warsaw We've got you covered! We'll help with relocation and paperwork, and you'll get free language lessons. Enjoy comprehensive health insurance, generous maternity and paternity leave, and flexible office hours. Plus, have fun with team outings and informal group activities throughout the year!

Be part of our incredible journey ahead! Join us now and help shape our bright future!
